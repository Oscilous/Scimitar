# Assembly Instructions

For assembly, cyanoacrylate (CA) glue (or an equivalent adhesive) can be used for bonding PLA parts, with mechanical fastening handled through bolts and nuts. During the build, carbon-fiber rods may require cutting and shaping, so appropriate protective equipment should be used as needed, and any soldering operations should be performed in a well-ventilated area. Since the parts are produced on consumer-grade FDM printers, all prints should be inspected for defects and fully cleaned of any support material. Finally, any surface intended for bonding with tape or epoxy should be thoroughly cleaned beforehand to ensure proper adhesion.

## Permanent Structures

1. Glue *Fuselage 3* to *Fuselage 4*. This forms the primary structural shell.
2. Glue *Fuselage 2* to *Fuselage 3*.
3. Glue *Fuselage 5* to *Fuselage 4*.
4. Glue *Wing L 1* and *Wing R 1* to the dedicated cutout on *Fuselage 3* and *Fuselage 4*.
5. Cut out 6 of the hinges from *Hinges TPU*.
6. Glue each hinge inside the designated cutouts on *Wing L 2* and *Wing R 2*.
7. Glue *Wing R Aileron* to the respective hinges on *Wing R 2*.
8. Glue *Wing L Aileron* to the respective hinges on *Wing L 2*.
9. Glue servo motors to *Wing L 2* and *Wing R 2* respectively.
10. Glue *Fuselage 1* to *Fuselage 2*.
11. Glue *Canopy handle* to *Canopy* using the designated hole.


## Final Assembly

1. Attach a motor to *Fuselage 6* using four M3x8 bolts.
2. Attach the *Fuselage 6* assembly to *Fuselage 5* using a M3x6 bolt.
3. Cut the 6 mm carbon tube to a length of 330 mm.
4. Insert the cut 6 mm carbon tube inside the 6 mm hole found on the fuselage assembly.
5. Cut the 8 mm carbon tube to a length of 500 mm.
6. Insert the cut 8 mm carbon tube inside the 8 mm hole found on the fuselage assembly.
7. Align the rods to guide the attachment of *Wing L 2* to *Wing L 1* and glue them together.
8. Align the rods to guide the attachment of *Wing R 2* to *Wing R 1* and glue them together.
9. Attach the canopy assembly to the fuselage body. Attachment is achieved by slightly bending the canopy during installation.
10. Put a M3 nut inside the designated area of *Wing L 2* and *Wing R 2*.
11. Attach *Wing L 3* to *Wing L 2* using an M3x8 bolt.
12. Attach *Wing R 3* to *Wing R 2* using an M3x8 bolt.
